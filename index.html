To use the dice roller, simply type in your dice quantity and size split by a "d". (ex: 4d6)
You can also add different types of dice together (ex: 4d6+1d20+2d8).
Or you can even use it as a calculator! (ex: 5-4+10)
You can also make the dice "explode" by adding an ! after the die. (ex: 4d6!)
Or drop a dice by putting another d and your desired number of dropped dice afterwards. (ex: 4d6d1)
